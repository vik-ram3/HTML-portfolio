Best Movies According To Vikram

My Top Three Movies All Time

Your Name

Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies. Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.

Silent Voice

The movie explores the depression of the protagonist that stems from the guilt of his past actions. It affects him in such a way that even years later, he has completely given up on life and would rather commit suicide than live.

Fight Club

Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless soap salesman. Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.
